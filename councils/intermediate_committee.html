United Nations Security Council (UNSC)

TOPIC 1: Yemen Civil War

TOPIC 2: Military Intervention by Member States

In the UNSC chambers in New York, a rising phoenix is flanked by images of war and discord. Beneath its facade of a peaceful society, our world is plagued with the dark penumbras of conflict – problems which only the UNSC, one of the few legally binding organs of the UN, can solve. Delegates can expect lively yet engaging debate on geopolitical issues, treading the fine line between principle and practicality – for the ostensible purpose of achieving a harmonious world. As such, the UNSC Dais welcomes you to this mission, an almost insurmountable task: resolving the Yemeni conflict, as well as navigating the minefield of political and diplomatic contentions in terms of standards of intervention by the UNSC.
